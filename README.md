# Sales-and-Profitability-Analysis-EXCEL-Project
#Short Description
An end-to-end data analytics and business intelligence project that transforms over 60,000 raw transactional records into an executive-ready performance ecosystem. This model structures fragmented retail data to track core KPIs, evaluate global market penetration across six nations, and uncover seasonal revenue trends—providing stakeholders with clear, data-driven pathways to optimize profit margins.

#Tech Stack
Core Analytics Platform: Microsoft Excel (Advanced Pivot Architecture, Data Validation, and Logical Formulas)

ETL & Data Engineering: Power Query / Excel Data Engine (Data extraction, schema formatting, and structural transformations)

Methodology: Relational Data Modeling (Fact vs. Dimension mapping) and Time-Series Analytics


#Data Source
Primary Dataset: A robust database containing 60,000+ historical transactional lines, product categorical tracking, and regional customer demographic profiles modeled after the enterprise AdventureWorks schema.

Core Reference Files: Sales and Profitability Analysis EXCEL.xlsx (incorporating structured sub-modules such as Rawsalesdata.csv, Fact_internet_sales_new.csv, and executive KPI frameworks) and Excelr


#Features and Highlights
⚙️ Data Engineering & ETL Pipeline (Features)
Automated Date Key Transformation: Engineered an ETL process to ingest raw, unformatted integer date stamps (e.g., 20121231) and automatically parse them into dynamic, readable attributes including Month Names, Days of the Week, and Financial Quarters.

Calculated Metric Architecture: Developed structural formulas to automatically derive business-critical columns—such as exact Production Cost, Profit, and Profit Margin %—directly within the transactional tables.

Star-Schema Structural Logic: Consolidated flat data dumps into an organized data model, effectively separating transactional metrics (Fact_internet_sales_new.csv) from descriptive lookups.

Executive Query Framework: Formulated a structured, question-based analysis layout (spanning analytical modules Q7 through Q12) allowing users to query specific business performance questions instantly.




📈 #Business Highlights & Data Discoveries (Highlights)

High-Value Financial Footprint: Successfully tracked and mapped an enterprise footprint totaling $29.36 Million in Revenue and $12.08 Million in Total Profit.

Premium Margin Benchmarking: Isolated an exceptional average organizational Profit Margin of 53.35% across core portfolios.

Multi-Category Performance Segmentation: Maintained complete operational visibility by breaking down metrics across three core retail lines: Bikes, Accessories, and Clothing.

Global Market Distribution: Evaluated geospatial revenue streams and audience penetrations across six major international territories: Australia, Canada, France, Germany, the United Kingdom, and the United States.

Temporal Trend Monitoring: Synthesized historical timelines into Year, Quarter, and Month buckets to isolate macro seasonal trends and predictable demand spikes.

Granular Audience Profiling: Surface critical target demographics by extracting the Top 5 Customers by sales value and order volume, while segmenting performance by regional gender distributions.

Consolidated KPI Dashboarding: Rolled up granular data points into an executive summary view (Q 12 KPI.csv), providing a single-screen pulse check on high-level operational health.
